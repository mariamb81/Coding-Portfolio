This site is a guide to all things related to biology and the study of life forms on Earth.

Biology is the study of all living things, from microorganisms to giant plants and animals.
Biology gives understanding to the many functions that make life possible on Earth, including the inner workings of your body and how plants grow.
Discovery awaits you as you study this amazing branch of science and thank you for visiting this site!
